Milk protein concentrate, cane sugar, fat reduced cocoa powder.

This all-natural milk protein formula contains whey and casein both. During workout, the muscles break and protein is needed to repair them. Whey is a fast-acting protein so provides an immediate flow of amino acids into the blood for muscle building. Since muscles burn calories even at rest so increase in muscle mass also increases the body’s metabolism and thus decreases the fat mass. The concentrate is also rich in micro fractions which helps to trigger fat loss. Protein also has a thermogenic effect which means you burn more calories by eating protein as compared to carbohydrates and fat, thus, triggering fat loss.

Casein is a slow acting protein so provides a continuous stream of amino acids for a prolonged period and helps you to feel full thus triggering fat loss. Casein also suppresses the hunger hormone, ghrelin, thus helps to provide satiety and aiding in weight loss.
